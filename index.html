Bio

Inspired by youthful sailing adventures in Greece, my art celebrates nature and heritage, aiming to stir emotions and spark conversations about preservation. I've exhibited in 12 group shows, including "In Praise of the Olive" a prestigious touring exhibition showcased in Greece and the United Nations in New York. I've also curated two solo exhibitions, delving into themes like the beauty of wooden fishing boats to the enduring legacy of ancient olive trees, showcasing the diverse shapes embedded within their weathered trunks.
